Research Interests

My current research interest lies in the development of information systems to increasing the efficiency and effectiveness of agricultural processes. In partnership with Rural Development Institute of Paraná (IDR, previous EMATER), EMBRAPA Soya, and National Service for Rural Learning (SENAR/PR), I have been focusing on two main topics:

1) Automating the integrated pest & disease management collection and analysis processes. Revista do Campo recently published report on project results.

2) Improving the communication of products & other information related to soya.

Other research interest include the use of experimentation to investigate the impact of GoF design patterns on software maintainability.
